Bija Training Android App

Bija Training

January 2019 - March 2019

The Bija Training Android App is developed to connect the organization's trainers and the staffs. In the app the staff can assign the trainers a specific appointment. It can either be a fresh one or a follow-up of an older appointment. The trainer, after receiving the notification about the task assigned, can either accept or decline the request. The staff will then be notified with the response of the trainer and can reschedule the appointment. Java and XML are used and MySQL was used for Database.
